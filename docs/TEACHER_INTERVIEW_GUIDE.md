# Teacher & Student Interview Guide
## AITutor Pilot — Week 1 Validation
## Government School — Class 8 & 9 Tamil

---

## Purpose
Validate our assumptions BEFORE writing more code.
30-minute conversations with 2–3 Tamil teachers and 4–5 students.

---

## Teacher Interview Questions

### Section A: Current Teaching Reality
1. How many students do you typically have in one Tamil class period?
2. How much time do you spend on actual concept teaching vs. classroom management?
3. Which topics in Class 8/9 Tamil syllabus do students find most difficult?
4. When a student doesn't understand something, what do you do currently?
5. Do students watch KalviTV videos? On their own phone or school TV?

### Section B: Digital Readiness
6. Do your students have smartphones? (Estimate: what % of class 8 & 9?)
7. Is there WiFi in school or nearby? What's connectivity like?
8. Do students have data packs? Do they use YouTube regularly?
9. Have you tried any digital tools for teaching Tamil before?

### Section C: Feedback on Our Concept
10. [Show Screen 3 — Lesson page mockup] What do you think about this?
11. [Show Screen 5 — AI Tutor chat] Would students type in Tamil? Or prefer voice?
12. Do you worry students will feel the AI is "replacing" you?
13. What would make you trust and use this tool in your classroom?
14. Would you check student progress on your phone or need a separate report?

### Section D: Content Priorities
15. Which Class 8 Tamil units should we start with? Why?
16. Which Class 9 topics need the most support?
17. Are there any KalviTV videos you already recommend to students?
18. What type of assessment do you find most valuable — written, oral, or mixed?

## Student Interview Questions (Age-appropriate, Informal)

*(Conduct in Tamil — do not translate to English during conversation)*

### Warm-up
1. என்ன பாடம் பிடிக்கும்? ஏன்?
2. யூட்யூபில் என்ன பார்ப்பாய்?
3. தமிழ் பாடம் கஷ்டமாக இருக்கா? எந்த பகுதி?

### About Learning Style
4. வீட்டில் படிக்கும்போது என்ன செய்வாய்?
5. ஆசிரியர் புரிய வைக்கலன்னு தோணும்போது என்ன செய்வாய்?
6. நண்பனிடம் பாடம் கேட்பாயா? அது உதவுதா?

### About Technology
7. கைப்பேசியில் படிப்பதற்கு சம்மதிக்கிறாயா?
8. தமிழில் type செய்ய தெரியுமா? (Observe if they can)
9. Voice பேசுவது easy-ஆ, type பண்றது easy-ஆ?

### Concept Validation
10. [Show AI tutor chat screen] இந்த app பயன்படுத்துவாயா?
11. இது ஒரு tutor மாதிரி பேசினா உதவுமா?
12. என்ன reward கிடைச்சா மகிழ்ச்சியா இருப்பே? (badge, points, certificate?)

## Key Assumptions to Validate

| Assumption | How to Test | Decision if Wrong |
|------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Students can type in Tamil | Ask them to type one sentence | Add voice-first UI |
| Students have smartphones | Ask teacher + students | Add school tablet mode |
| KalviTV videos are familiar | Ask if they've watched any | Find other video source |
| Students will use app daily | Ask about homework habits | Design for 3x/week use |
| Teachers will check dashboards | Show teacher screen | Simplify to weekly WhatsApp report |
| Badges motivate students | Ask what rewards they want | Adjust gamification |

---

## What to Observe (Don't Just Ask)

- Watch a student try to type in Tamil on their phone
- Watch a teacher try to navigate the mockup (note where they get confused)
- Notice classroom infrastructure: fans, lighting, board type, phone charging points
- Check if school has a computer lab or smart TV already
